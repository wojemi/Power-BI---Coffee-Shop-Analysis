# Power-BI---Coffee-Shop-Analysis
The aim of the project is to identify key conclusions regarding sales in the coffee shop. The analysis was conducted using Power BI.

# Business problem
This project aims to identify key insights regarding sales, products, product categories in the coffee shop. Dataset comes from Kaggle website and contains:
- transaction date
- product name
- product category
- transaction time
- price and many more

# Tools
- Power BI
- Excel

# Business questions
1. What is the minimum, maximum and average sales?
2. What is the amount of products by each category?
3. How many transactions are made at different times of the day?
4. How are sales growing month-to-month?

# Key insights
1. In March sales increased, but fell dramatically in June.
2. In the second quarter of the year sales are bigger than in the first.
3. Most people buys products in the morning - probably on their way to school or work.
4. Most frequently purchased products by category: Tea, Coffee, Bakery.
